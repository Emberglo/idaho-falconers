Welcome To The Idaho Falconers Association!

The IFA

The Idaho Falconers Association was founded in 1978 in order to advocate for the sport of falconry and the falconers of Idaho. The IFA is involved in the conservation of birds of prey and the education of the public about raptors and falconry. The IFA is also active and involved in advocating for all Idaho hunters with the Idaho Sportsman's Caucus.

Falconry As A Cultural Heritage

In 2016, Falconry was declared an Intangible Cultural Heritage by UNESCO. This recognizes the long history of falconry throughout multiple cultures around the world.
